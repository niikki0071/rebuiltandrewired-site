About

I didn't plan to rebuild. Life made the decision for me.

I spent over a decade in healthcare technology — translating massive, chaotic corporate systems into paint-by-numbers workflows that eliminated decision fatigue for entire hospital networks. I trained hundreds of people who swore they "couldn't do tech" and turned them into power users.

Then I lost my career overnight.

ADHD brain that was misdiagnosed for decades as anxiety. Bills that didn't care about my healing timeline.

So I did what I've always done with chaos: I built a system.

Not a vision board. Not affirmations. Not a 47-module video course I'd never finish. I built a real, step-by-step method for going from stuck to earning — designed specifically for brains that don't work the way traditional business advice assumes.

That system became the REWIRE Method.

Now I run three businesses. Still have ADHD. The difference is the system — the same system I put into The Restart Blueprint.

Why Rebuilt & Rewired exists

Most business advice is written for people with strong executive function — the ability to plan, prioritize, start tasks, and follow through without external structure.

If you have ADHD, if you're operating under extreme stress, if your brain is still recovering from a major life disruption — that advice fails you. And then you blame yourself for not being able to follow it.

Your brain isn't broken. Your systems are.

Rebuilt & Rewired builds the systems your brain actually needs. Practical. No-BS. Built by someone who's been on the floor and got back up.
